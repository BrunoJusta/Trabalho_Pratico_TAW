The Krystal Jewellery Story

In 2007 Krystal Jewellery was born. We began selling semi-precious components combined with beautiful charms. In 2008 we launched our first concession in Topshop and we've been growing ever since, with retail partners across the globe and an international website. We travel far and wide to find inspiration for beautiful jewellery and deliver it to our loyal customers with the signature Krystal touch.
